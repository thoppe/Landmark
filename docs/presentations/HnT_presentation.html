What is a cryptocurrency?

A cryptographic protocol to secure transactions and verify the transfer of assets. Bitcoin ($115 B) and Ethereum ($32 B) are examples.



What is a blockchain?

A public ledger that records transactions. To enforce trust, everybody can download and verify the entire chain.



What is Ethereum?

A cryptocurrency that stores on its blockchain both values and can run code.
